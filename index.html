About African Kaya

African Kaya is a community-driven organization dedicated to environmental conservation and sustainable development across the African continent.

Our Vision

We envision an Africa where communities live in harmony with nature, where environmental stewardship is woven into the fabric of our cultures, and where future generations inherit a thriving, sustainable continent.

Founded on the principles of Ubuntu - "I am because we are" - African Kaya brings together volunteers, communities, and partners to create lasting environmental change.

Success Story: The Green Village Project

In rural Kenya, we transformed a struggling community into a model of sustainability. Through Operation Goodwill, we implemented:

  • Community-led tree planting restoring 50 hectares of degraded land
  • Waste-to-resource program creating local employment
  • Environmental education integrated into school curriculum
